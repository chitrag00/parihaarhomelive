We are a Social Entrepreneurship.
Building a mobile based solution for the healthcare industry, we want to touch the lives of RA patients by making available features and services that make their lives easier. Founded by a patient and a Rheumatologist, our mission is to give continuous support to patients with chronic arthritis conditions.

Vision

Parihaar aims to act as a platform to bring together the practitioners of various systems as well as branches of medicine in the best interests of the patients suffering from RA.

Mission

Our mission is to give continuous support to patients with chronic arthritis conditions. Through the Parihaar app resources for self help, awareness, tips for daily living, exercise chart and tracking we want arthritis patients to live and thrive.

Our Offerings

Subscription based helpline for people with RA for in between clinics support.
In app consultation feature, where you can initiate a consultation through the app to your consulting Rheumatologist.
Features that enables patient driven adherence to treatment.
Features for Outcome based care.
